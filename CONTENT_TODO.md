## Asset Submission Guidelines

**File Naming Convention:**
- Photos: `photo-[category]-[description]-[date].jpg`
  - Example: `photo-campus-entrance-20250119.jpg`
- Videos: `video-[type]-[name]-[date].mp4`
  - Example: `video-testimonial-ahmad-20250119.mp4`
- Projects: `project-[name]-[year].zip`
  - Example: `project-ecommerce-2024.zip`

**Submission Location:**
- Store in: `raw-content/assets/`
- Subdirectories: `/photos`, `/videos`, `/projects`, `/logos`

**Metadata Required:**
Each asset should include:
- Date captured/created
- Subjects (who is in the photo/video)
- Permission status (release form signed Y/N)
- Usage rights (unlimited, limited to website, etc.)
